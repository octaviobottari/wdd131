Site Plan: CanchasApp

A platform for finding and booking soccer fields, tailored for soccer enthusiasts in Argentina.

Navigation Example: Home | Listings | Booking

Site Name

CanchasApp

The name was chosen because it reflects the app's purpose clearly: helping users book soccer fields (*canchas*). The name is short, catchy, and relates directly to soccer, a cultural cornerstone in Argentina.

Optional Domain: canchasapp.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of CanchasApp is to make it easy for users to find and book soccer fields in their area. It aims to provide detailed field listings (location, price, availability) and a streamlined reservation system to simplify the process for users.

Scenarios

  • How can I find soccer fields near my location?
  • What are the prices and availability of soccer fields in Buenos Aires?

Color Schema

  • Red (#da2c38): Used for headings.
  • Dark Green (#226f54): Used for navigation bar and accents.
  • Soft Green (#87c38f): Used for highlights.
  • Light Beige (#f4f0bb): Used as the background for the site.
  • Dark Brown (#43291f): Used for text and footer content.

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif

  • Headings: Bold, Red (#da2c38)
  • Body Text: Regular, Dark Brown (#43291f)
